Who We Are

Lucania Contractors LLC is a heavy civil construction firm located in South Texas, specializing in providing top-tier infrastructure solutions for the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) and several municipalities statewide.

We are committed to delivering exceptional construction services, including roadways, bridges, drainage systems, and other essential public works projects — safely, on schedule, and built to last.
